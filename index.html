About

Words that root, stories that grow.

Hi, I'm Audrey — an email marketer and content creator with a love for language that feels honest and alive.

I'm a graduate of Dalhousie University, and over the years I've built my practice around a simple belief: the best marketing doesn't shout — it listens. It meets people where they are, offers something useful or beautiful, and leaves them feeling a little more connected than before.

Whether I'm writing a welcome series, shaping a brand's voice, or dreaming up a content calendar, I bring the same intention I bring to my yoga practice — presence, patience, and a genuine curiosity for what makes each story unique.
